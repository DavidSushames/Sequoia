A dualistic pantheon consisting of the sun, and the void beyond the stars.

The [[Sun]] is the beginning. It is life and light and warmth. Where all things originated. It gives life so you can live until you die and your body continues to feed into the cycle of life and death.

The [[Void]] beyond the rim of the world is the end. It is death and darkness and cold. Where all things will eventually cease to be. When the time is right the world will be consumed by the void.
